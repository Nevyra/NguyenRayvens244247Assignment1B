What is a Living Style Guide

In the world of programming, the process of documentation is often the weakest point for a programmer, there are often times where it is the most essential part of a project for a programmer. due to it being a reference point for developers and designers, the Living Style Guide comes in handy for useful information such as button themes, logos, layouts, overviews and colour palettes.

Why is a Living Style Guide useful to developers and designers

Living Style Guides are useful to developers and designers as they act as a tool that helps them convey different design languages, define rationale and understand the usuage of markup. A Living Style Guides changes and adapts to new needs and challenges that arise for the team during the project. TeamGasLight

My list of Living Style Guide tips

    1. Allow for critisism and open ideas, putting ideas onto the table will always help in improving the current project stages.
    2. Dont try to make everything perfect, always be open to different scenarios and try to make it work. good things arent made in one go.
    3. Make sure you have the essential elements: information about logos, colour palettes, have an overview of the project and always eager to work rationally.
    4. Create a easy to read step by step guide, preferbly with photos or other infographics that will help with understanding the steps.
